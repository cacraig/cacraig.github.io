Ngwips - The Next Generation Weather Model Analysis Tool

Basically, Ngwips strives to
simplify and accelerate forecasting.

With Ngwips, forecasters and weather fanatics alike can seamlessly visualize a variety of weather analyses.

It gives users the ultimate freedom in building, and sharing weather analysis charts. Additionally, users can easily share thier analyses with others. Best of all it's Free!
